About Me

My name is Antonin Cheymol, I hold a Master in Human Computer Interaction, received in October 2020, from the Paris-Saclay University (France). I plan to do a PhD at the French research institute INRIA Rennes in the Hybrid Team on the theme of “body-based interfaces” from November 2021.

Eager to acquire new research and cultural experiences, I decided to conduct different research oversea between my Master graduation and the start of my PhD. I am currently intern student at the Korea university (South Korea) in the Media Lab for 6 months. Before I also worked 1 year at the University of Tokyo (Japan) in the Cyber Interface Lab as a research student.

My research interests are mainly focus on improving and expanding the usages of mixed realities. To this end, I am particularly interested in the understanding and exploitation of human perception in order to develop novel interactions and experiences, specific to mixed realities.
